    Minecarft Mobs

    Creeper
    A creeper is a fictional creature within the sandbox game Minecraft.
    Creepers are hostile mobs (a mobile non-player character) that can spawn anywhere that is dark,
    but can still survive in bright environments.
    Zombie
    Zombies are common undead hostile mobs that deal
    melee damage and commonly attack in groups.
    Skeleton
    A skeleton is an undead hostile mob that performs
    ranged attacks with a bow.
    Spider
    A spider is a neutral mob that attacks the player in dark areas.
    Spiders attack by biting the target,
    and they can also climb walls and lunge at them.
